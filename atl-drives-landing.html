Process

Up and running
in four steps.

01

Build your profile

Enter your home, work, and any recurring stops. Set your typical days and pickup times. This becomes your default schedule — change it anytime.

Review your upcoming week. Modify timing, add a stop, or cancel — all from your passenger portal. No phone calls. No back-and-forth.
